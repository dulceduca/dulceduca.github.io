Um Belo Dia

A Beautiful Day

Creation: 2011

Emotional Comedy Show

An oniric, metaphoric and surrealistic piece, using physical Theatre, circus, dance, juggling/ balancing of clubs. With live music. For all audiences.

UM BELO DIA is a red pepper, on to which you put wings and it becomes a butterfly.

Comedia emocional

Una pieza onírica, metafórica y surrealista, que utiliza el teatro físico, circo, danza, malabarismo / equilibrio de mazas. Para todos los públicos. UM BELO DIA es un pimiento rojo, al que le pones alas y se convierte en una mariposa.
