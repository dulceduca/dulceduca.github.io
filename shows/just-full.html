Just Full

Premiered Festival Rencontres des Jongleurs, Paris, France
Creation: 2019
Duration: 9 minutes
For: Cabarets | Shows | Events

An absurd & intimate juggling act.

A lady in full decadence. She is there, adomed with her clubs, her dress her underwear, her high-heeled shoes. She throws it all. Yes, this woman is a juggler.

We do not know where she comes from, where she is going, if she likes chocolate, if she is a feminist or not. # Just full is about when you touch the bottom, you know that from your heel you will not go lower.
