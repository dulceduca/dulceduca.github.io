Unstoppable

New show premiered at the 1st edition Bartholomew Fair, City of London, September 2023
Duration: 30 minutes
Woman duet in an Intense and Comic Street Show

A contemporary and absurd street show for a small and medium scale.

Hilarious and shocking, it combines roller skate juggling and street theatre in an outrageous and highly original comedy.

Two women (one in her 40s and the other in her 70s). One is there to perform her show, to be adored and the other is her mother who is there to help and support her daughter, but is also uncomfortable with her daugther's cultural expression activities. The two, with different personalities and an extreme attitude problem, confront each other involving everything and everyone around them.

Original idea: Dulce Duca Writing: Joe Mackintosh Direction: Dulce Duca Interpretation: Dulce Duca & Sarah Munro Original Soundtrack: Tam Cooper & Joe Mackintosh Costumes: Sarah Munro & Tsubi Du Financed by: The Arts Council England Produced by: DULCE DUCA & Out There Arts National Center for Street Arts and Circus Co-produced by: Fiar (PT), VaudeVille Rendez Vous Festival (PT) Creation support: The Albani (UK), La Central del Circo (ES), Festival Trengo (PT)
