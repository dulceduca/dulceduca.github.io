Is a Pistacatro Prodution, Galicia, Spain

Juggling Orchestra is a performance that features six jugglers and a municipal band. A relationship between music and circus. A concert where the jugglers are the dancers of this aerial ballet. Clarinetes, saxophones and trumpets are the painters of an expressionist painting in which the balls, maces and hoops draw in the air the feelings and emotions conveyed by the music and the circus.
